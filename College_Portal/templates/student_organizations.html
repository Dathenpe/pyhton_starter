Student Organizations

Our college boasts a vibrant community with a wide array of student organizations. Getting involved is a fantastic way to explore new interests, develop leadership skills, meet like-minded peers, and significantly enhance your college experience.

Categories of Organizations

Academic & Professional

Clubs aligned with specific majors or career paths, offering networking, mentorship, and skill-building opportunities essential for professional development.

  • Computer Science Club
  • Pre-Med Society
  • Debate Team

Cultural & Identity

Groups celebrating diverse cultures, backgrounds, and identities through engaging events, discussions, and community gatherings, fostering an inclusive campus.

  • International Students Association
  • African-American Cultural Society
  • Pride Alliance

Arts & Performance

Opportunities to express creativity through music, drama, visual arts, and more, offering a vibrant outlet for artistic talents.

  • Theater Group
  • A Cappella Choir
  • Photography Club

Service & Activism

Clubs focused on community service, advocacy, and making a positive impact through various initiatives and outreach programs.

  • Volunteer Network
  • Environmental Action Group
  • Human Rights Advocates

How to Join

Getting involved is easy! Attending an involvement fair, visiting club meetings, or reaching out directly to club leaders are great ways to get started. Most organizations welcome new members throughout the year, so there's always an opportunity to find your place.
