On-Campus Housing Options

Our college offers a variety of on-campus housing options designed to provide a comfortable, safe, and engaging living environment for our students. Living on campus is a fantastic way to immerse yourself in college life, build strong connections, and enhance your overall academic journey.

Residence Halls

North Hall

Traditional style dorms, popular for first-year students. Features shared rooms, common lounges, and communal bathrooms. Known for its vibrant community feel and welcoming atmosphere.

East Suites

Suite-style living with private bedrooms and a shared living area/bathroom within the suite. Ideal for upperclassmen seeking more privacy and communal space.

University Apartments

Apartment-style living with full kitchens and private or shared bedrooms. Available for graduate students and older undergraduates, offering independent living experience.

Amenities & Services

  • 24/7 Security and Resident Assistants (RAs) ensuring a safe and supportive environment.
  • High-speed Wi-Fi access available throughout all residential areas.
  • Convenient laundry facilities on each floor/building for easy access.
  • Comfortable study lounges and common areas designed for collaboration and relaxation.
  • On-site dining options with flexible meal plans to suit various dietary needs.
  • Prompt maintenance services to address any facility concerns.

Application Process

Housing applications typically open in Spring for the upcoming academic year. Early application is highly recommended due to limited availability and high demand for on-campus living.
